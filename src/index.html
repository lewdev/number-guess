What is this?

A Number Guessing Game with score board that tracks time and number of guesses. Also features multiple difficulties.

Why?

I have always wanted to just build something and I was caught up in trying to build things with unique ideas. I finally just thought I should just build something simple and easy in the beginning. This helped me get started and motivated enough to complete one product. It was in a usable state after my first night of development.
